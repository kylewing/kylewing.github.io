16.1.02 - Learn as you go...

Google is your friend

Kahn Academy is fantastic don't get me wrong. I was put on to Sal and his physics videos by my AP physics teacher, my sophomore year in high school. Since then he's gotten me through AP Physics, AP Calculus AB and BC, Honors Chem, AP Bio, etc, etc. The philosophy that education should be free and accessible to everyone is something I wholeheartedly believe in. I've already donated. I love them.

That being said, I almost don't know what else they can give me. So much of this seems to be learn as you go and google when you have questions. There are so many references and documentation that this is really something, I believe, that everyone can, and maybe even should learn.

That's all I've got to say now. Not really substantive. Moral is, use Kahn Academy, then set yourself free and dive in. The internet is your friend when trying to learn about the internet.

Keep on keeping on,
K.Wing
